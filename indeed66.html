    • India - Driving Education Empowerment and Development Foundation (INDEED Foundation) is focused on improving quality of education, learning outcomes and academic performance, supporting character development and personality enhancement of school children in rural India enabling them to secure decent livelihoods leading happy and content lives

    The Foundation hopes to help students become self reliant, empowered, technology ready, exposed to new-age opportunities, confident and prepared to take up various vocational skill-development courses offered by the government and other private institutions for gainful employment.

    • Youth Volunteerism

    • Skill Enhancement

      1. Computer / IT skills training

      2. Communication and Spoken English

      3. Personality development





    • Student Exchange

      1. Public school students to visit rural government schools and reciprocal visits by rural students to premier public schools in the cities

      2. Extra curricular activities like drawing, painting, debating, essay writing

      3. Peer-learning and problem solving

      1. Experience sharing, capacity building sessions, mentoring and motivation by successful alumni

    Trustees

    • Vikram Rajola

      Chairman

      Vikram is a development professional with work experience in several Asian countries like Afghanistan, Cambodia, China, India, Indonesia, Nepal, Philippines and Singapore. He holds a Masters degree in Environmental Engineering from the Delhi Technological University (DTU). During the past 15 years he has worked with Government bodies, leading Universities, Funding Agencies and International NGOs across Asia. From 2007 to 2011 he worked as a development manager at the prestigious Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, one of the top 100 Universities in the World.

    • Avinash Mathur

      Vice-Chairman

      Avinash Mathur, a civil engineer with around two decades of field experience is presently the General Manager of a large real estate firm. He is the go-to man for all purposes whether it is related to developing proposals, implementing programs, long-term strategy building for the Foundation or routine day-to-day issues. A core member of the Founding team he brings his practical experience and clarity of vision to bear upon all aspects of INDEED Foundation.

    • Capt. G.R. Choudhary

      Secretary

      G.R. Choudhary is the Secretary of INDEED Foundation. He brings more than 45 years of experience in man-management, having served in the Indian Army and the State Bank of India. He was commissioned in the 9th Gorkha Rifles in 1971 and saw action on the Western front during the Indo Pak war where he was wounded in action and was a POW.

    • Raja Brijbhushan

      Board Member & Trustee

      Raja Brijbhushan is a serial entrepreneur who has experimented with ventures in the field of software services, organic farming and pre-primary school education. An engineer by training and a technology enthusiast, he has a patented technology in the field of image processing and process automation. Even during his student life his talents were recognized and awarded at various forums.

    • Sitesh Prasad

      Board Member & Trustee

      An electrical engineer from Delhi College of Engineering and an MBA in Marketing and Finance from Symbiosis, Pune. He has diverse experience across multiple sectors including Engineering, Financial Services, Insurance and Hospitality.In his 20 years of service, he has set up new businesses and channels, led large teams across geographies and managed relations with regulatory authorities during the course of work.

    • Jitendra Dagur

      Board Member & Trustee

      Jitendra Dagur is Director, IT at CA Technologies Hyderabad India. Having graduated in computer engineering from Engineering College Kota, he has in the past worked with prestigious companies like IBM. He has always been passionate about supporting worthy causes contributing his time and efforts in various volunteering assignments across the country.Jitendra helps INDEED Foundation in resource raising and spreading the good word to network and raise the profile of the organization.
